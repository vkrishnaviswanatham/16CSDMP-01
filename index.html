Empowering Handyman

The idea is to provide a framework for medium connecting people who are looking to hire workers.

Objective

There are many illiterates who have good skill in carpentry, farming, driving, etc but unable to find work. Skilled people who are searching for work need to specify their skill and phone number on our platform. Those who want to hire can contact workers through the phone number.

  • Search Hiree by SKILL
  • Search Hiree by EXPERIENCE
  • Search Hiree by AGE
  • Search Hiree by LOCATION

Hirer Panel

Hirer can select a location where he/she wish to hire by applying filters like skill,experince and age.
